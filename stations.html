Green Line Stations

The MBTA Green Line consists of multiple branches—B, C, D, and E—serving key destinations across the Boston area. It connects bustling hubs like Government Center and Park Street with neighborhoods such as Allston, Brookline, Newton, and Somerville. Major stops include North Station, Copley, Kenmore, and Lechmere, providing access to landmarks like Fenway Park, Boston University, and the Museum of Fine Arts. The line blends underground and street-level travel, offering a vital link across the city.

Services of MBTA Green Line

The MBTA Green Line runs 7 days a week, with late-night services during weekends. Accessibility services, including wheelchairs and live train tracking, could be found on the MBTA Mobile App.
